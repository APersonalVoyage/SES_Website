Our Services

Comprehensive steel fabrication solutions for critical infrastructure

Water Tank Tower Structures

Engineered elevated water storage systems designed for municipalities, industrial facilities, and commercial developments. Our water tank towers ensure reliable water supply with structural integrity built to last decades.

Key Offerings

  • Elevated Storage Tanks: Capacities from 50,000 to 500,000 liters with custom heights
  • Support Structures: Lattice towers, tubular columns, and shaft-type supports
  • Tank Platforms: Access platforms, ladders, and safety railings
  • Foundation Design: Complete foundation engineering and load calculations
  • Accessories: Level indicators, overflow systems, and maintenance access

Technical Specifications

  • Material: IS 2062 Grade steel with hot-dip galvanized finish
  • Design Standards: IS 875 (Wind Load), IS 1893 (Seismic)
  • Coating: 610 microns zinc coating (85 microns minimum)
  • Welding: Per IS 816 specifications
  • Service Life: 50+ years with proper maintenance

Solar Panel Mounting Structures

High-performance mounting systems for solar PV installations, engineered to withstand extreme weather while maximizing energy generation. Suitable for ground-mounted and rooftop applications.

Key Offerings

  • Ground-Mount Systems: Fixed tilt and adjustable angle structures for utility-scale projects
  • Rooftop Systems: Ballasted and penetrating mount solutions for commercial buildings
  • Tracking Systems: Single-axis tracker support structures for enhanced efficiency
  • Module Clamping: Rail systems and clamps compatible with all major panel brands
  • Cable Management: Integrated cable trays and grounding systems

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Pre-galvanized steel (120 GSM) or hot-dip galvanized
  • Design Standards: IS 6745, IS 875, AS/NZS 1170
  • Wind Speed: Designed for up to 180 kmph wind zones
  • Tilt Angle: 10° to 30° (customizable)
  • Corrosion Protection: 25-year warranty on galvanization

Electricity Board Pole Structures

Heavy-duty steel poles for power transmission and distribution networks, street lighting, and telecommunications infrastructure. Engineered for reliability in demanding utility applications.

Key Offerings

  • Transmission Poles: 11kV to 132kV high-tension line support structures
  • Distribution Poles: LT and HT distribution network poles with cross-arms
  • Street Light Poles: Decorative and standard poles for urban lighting (4m to 12m)
  • Telecom Towers: Monopoles and lattice towers for telecom equipment

Technical Specifications

  • Material: IS 2062/IS 1239 steel tubes and sections
  • Heights: 6m to 30m (customizable)
  • Design Load: As per electrical authority specifications
  • Finish: Hot-dip galvanized (minimum 85 microns)
  • Standards: IS 2713, CEA guidelines, state electricity board specs

Custom Steel Fabrication

Bespoke fabrication services for industrial and commercial projects requiring specialized steel structures. From concept to installation, we deliver engineered solutions tailored to your unique requirements.

Capabilities

  • Structural Steelwork: Building frames, mezzanines, platforms, and walkways
  • Industrial Equipment: Tanks, hoppers, conveyors, and process equipment structures
  • Architectural Metalwork: Canopies, facades, railings, and decorative elements
  • Heavy Fabrication: Beams, columns, trusses up to 20m length
  • Precision Components: CNC-cut parts and assemblies to tight tolerances

Process

  • Design consultation and engineering support
  • Material procurement and quality verification
  • CNC cutting, bending, and forming
  • Welding (MIG, TIG, Arc) per AWS/IS standards
  • Surface treatment and protective coatings
  • Quality inspection and delivery
