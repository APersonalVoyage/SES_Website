ABOUT
SHRI ENGINEERING & STEEL

SHRI ENGINEERING AND STEEL is a leading manufacturer and supplier of heavy-duty structural solutions for the renewable energy, water infrastructure, power transmission, and utility sectors. With extensive expertise in steel fabrication, we specialize in designing, fabricating, and installing robust, corrosion-resistant structures that meet global engineering standards.
Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, equipped with advanced machinery and a skilled workforce, ensures precision, durability, and cost-efficiency in every project. We are committed to delivering high-quality galvanized steel structures, ensuring longevity and superior performance in harsh environments.

OUR VISION

To be the most trusted provider of high-performance structural solutions, driving sustainable infrastructure development through innovation, precision engineering, and uncompromising quality in steel fabrication.

OUR MISSION

We design, fabricate, and deliver robust steel structures including water tank towers, solar mounting systems, and galvanized steel solutions that set industry benchmarks for durability, safety, and cost-efficiency. Through cutting-edge technology, skilled craftsmanship, and client-centric collaboration, we empower water infrastructure and renewable energy projects to achieve long-term reliability and excellence.

SHRI ENGINEERING & STEEL
WATER TANK TOWER STRUCTURES

We manufacture high-tensile, galvanized steel tower structures for elevated water tanks, designed to support water storage systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

Key Offerings

▶
Galvanized Steel Tower Structures – Heights ranging from 10m to 30m, compliant with IS 2713, IS 876 standards
▶
Custom Load-Bearing Designs – Engineered to support tanks from 5,000 to 100,000 liters capacity
▶
Anti-Corrosion Treatment – Hot-dip galvanization (minimum 80 microns coating) for long-term durability
▶
Seismic & Wind Load Resistance – Designed for Zone III, IV, V requirements as per IS 1893
▶
Complete Fabrication & Installation – Turnkey solutions from design to commissioning
SHRI ENGINEERING & STEEL
SOLAR PANEL MOUNTING STRUCTURES

We provide custom-engineered solar mounting systems for ground-mounted, rooftop, and pole-mounted installations, designed to withstand extreme weather conditions.

Key Offerings

▶
Hot-Dip Galvanized Steel Structures – Corrosion-resistant, 30+ years lifespan
▶
Adjustable & Fixed-Tilt Systems – Optimized for maximum sunlight exposure
▶
High Wind & Snow Load Resistance – Compliant with IS 800, IS 875, and IEC 61730
▶
Quick Installation & Low Maintenance – Pre-drilled holes for faster assembly
▶
Custom Fabrication – Tailored designs for utility-scale, commercial & residential projects
SHRI ENGINEERING & STEEL
ELECTRICITY BOARD POLE STRUCTURES

We manufacture high-tensile, galvanized steel poles for LT (Low Tension) & HT (High Tension) power distribution networks.

Key Offerings

▶
Galvanized Steel Poles – 8m to 20m height, compliant with IS 2713, IS 876
▶
Specialized Designs – Straight, tapered, and cantilever poles
▶
Anti-Corrosion Treatment – Hot-dip galvanization (minimum 80 microns coating)
▶
Custom Fabrication – As per state DISCOM & electricity board specifications
